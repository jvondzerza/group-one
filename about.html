Welcome to the Hot Boss

Hot boss is perhaps best known as the company that made the peppers big in Europe. Or perhaps as the pioneer behind the process of ripening exotic peppers and extending the shelf life, with Apeel. But we are so much more than that. We aim for long-term relationships: with growers, customers, and partners we are proud to work with. Sustainable collaboration is a precondition for us to be more than just a supplier. Together, we want to take the chain to the next level. We inspire each other to continue to develop innovations. Together, we make consumers even more enthusiastic about exotic fruit and vegetables. And we help growers to optimise their business operations. This is how we create better products together with growers and customers.

Our Mission

Provide our industry leading expertise for growing the world's most awarded hot peppers, natural farming practices and artisan recipes.

Our Promise

We will provide you great tasting, natural food from our farm to your table with a level of love and hospitality that you can't find anywhere else.

Our Essense

We farm and process all of our peppers from our farm and pack the most freshness and peppers into each bottle by far.
