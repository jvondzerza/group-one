Bird's Eye Chili

This chili pepper is very spicy, with Scoville scale 50,000-100,000 SHU. It is perfect to be used in soups, salads, sauces, pastes and marinades, as a condiment or eaten raw, fresh and dry.

Sugar Rush Chili Pepers

This rare Peach colored Capsicum bacattum is as spicy as the Bird's Eye Chili, with Scoville scale 50.000 - 100.000. The flavor of Sugar Rush Peach peppers is, of course, sweet. This means that the Sugar Rush Peach is a perfect ingredient for fruity salsa, or to spice up a bean chili dish.

Bradley Bahamian Pepper

This chili pepper is hotter than the cayenne, but not quite as hot as most habaneros, with Scoville scale 95,000-110,000 SHU. They make a delicious spicy addition to many foods and dishes.
